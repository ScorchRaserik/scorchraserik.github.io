Ryan is currently a web developer
and Team Lead at Ennovar at Wichita State University

At Ennovar, Ryan mainly focuses on front-end development using Angular 2 and Meteor.js (as well as basic HTML, CSS, and Javascript), but he is also tasked with some back-end development and QA testing for his team when needed. His day to day tasks range from developing full webpages (such as this one) from photoshop images, to smaller modules that will be integrated into multiple webpages (such as the various modules on this page).

Ryan has a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and a minor in Creative Writing from Wichita State University. For his senior project, he worked with three other seniors on a Touch-Enabled Vending Assistant, a device that fits over existing vending machines that audibly tells users where items are and the price of the items, in order to help blind and low-vision users.

In his very limited free time, Ryan develops small, personal games using Javascript, XNA, and Unity. He is also a moderator of the /r/StarWarsBattlefront subreddit and is an active member of the Star Wars: Battlefront II modding community.
